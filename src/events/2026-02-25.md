talkTitle: "Beyond Electron: High-Performance Desktop Apps with Rust and Tauri"
presenter: "Merrick Young"
---

In this talk, presenter Merrick Young will explore building high-performance, cross-platform desktop apps with Rust and Tauri. He will constrast it with Electron to show how one can get native feeling apps with dramatically smaller binaries.
